About the Brand

BY ARAFAT is a fashion and lifestyle brand built around one idea: BECOMING We believe fashion is more than clothing—it is expression, confidence, and identity in motion. It is how a woman shows up as who she is and who she is becoming. Our mission is simple: to make affordable luxury fashion accessible to women who want to elevate their style and presence without breaking the bank. From university girls to working women and creatives, BY ARAFAT exists for women ready to step into a better-dressed, more intentional version of themselves

FOUNDER
